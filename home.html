Sharing And caring charity foundation

The caring and sharing foundation aims to help the Gambian children fully realize their dreams. We want to see a smile on every child's face. We help children have a quality edication, good health standard, clean environmentn, good shelter and good quality food. Children happiness is always our concern!

Sharing and Caring foundation is founded purposely to educate, motivate and improve the future of The Gambians.

Our vision is to create a good platforms for The Gambian children to learn in a very conducive environment.

  • To create a conducive environment for all children to stay in peace and with knowledge

why we help

There are many less fortunate people in this world. If anyone can, they should lend a helping hand. We think it's very meaningful to share your feelings and compassion through giving.

Our focus

We focus on developing children by unlocking their full potential in both practical and theoretical mindset.

Empowering kids in skills development and give them chances and confidence to choose on their own.

Providing a conducive environment for quality education and develop a good collaboration and a sprite of teamwork.

Providing quality food and good shelter for children and give them a good platform to explore their talent.

We make sure and ensure that every child wears clean and decent cloth and also expresses themselves freely.

We give them the platforms to build a boost their confidence by organizing events and talent shows for them.
